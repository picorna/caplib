The following PDF files are for making paper models of icosahedral virus capsids,
adopting the tiling theory of virus (see for example, Twarock & Luque, 
Nature communications, 2019. ).

hexagonaltiling.pdf	for the hexagonal tiling
trihexagonaltiling.pdf	for the trihexagonal tiling
dualoftrihexagonal.pdf	for the dual of the trihexagonal tiling
